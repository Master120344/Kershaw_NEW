Our Commitment to You

At The Kershaw Law Firm, P.C., we are driven by integrity, thorough legal knowledge, and a steadfast dedication to our clients. We assist U.S. businesses in managing the H-2A (agricultural) and H-2B (non-agricultural) temporary foreign worker visa programs.

Our legal team diligently works through the detailed regulations from the Department of Labor (DOL) and U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS). We prepare your petitions accurately and aim for efficient processing, recognizing that timely workforce solutions are vital for your business.
